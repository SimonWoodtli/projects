Panda

A panda can poop 28 Kilos per day!

  • Scientific Name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Average Size: 1.2 - 1.9 meters
  • Average Weight: 70 - 125 kg
  • Average Lifespan: 20 - 30 years

The giant panda is a bear native to south central China. It is characterised by large, black patches around its eyes, over the ears, and across its round body.
